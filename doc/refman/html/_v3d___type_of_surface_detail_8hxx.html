enum V3d_TypeOfSurfaceDetail

Modes of visualization for objects in a view.

  • V3d_TEX_NONE: no texture mapping,
  • V3d_TEX_ENVIRONMENT: environment mapping only,
  • V3d_TEX_ALL: environment and texture mapping.
